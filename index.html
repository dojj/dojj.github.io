  • KPI Dashboard: Focus Actions on the Data That Matter

    HR Operations and I designed a course to help managers explore the KPI Dashboard.

    This course provides a comprehensive overview of the People Metrics, reporting and exporting functionality of the Dashboard along with exposure to the Scorecard and Executive Summary.

    Learning Objectives

      Helped users explore the functionality of the dashboard by showing how to:

    • Access the Dashboard
    • Navigate through the tabs of each dashboard section
    • Drill down to view the specific data required
    • Understand data that is related to People Metrics
    • Interpret and use this data to make business decisions

    Learner engagement

    Using xAPI, I was able to capture the number of likes, shares, and views to discover how learners are interacting with the content and learn how I can further optimize the learning experience.

  • Datix: Patient Safety and Risk Management

    The Risk Management team and I created a video tutorial on how to capture and assess important details in Datix, a web-based incident reporting and risk management service, to create a culture of patient safety.

    learning Objectives

    The goal was to show how to:

    • Access Datix
    • Use the Event Status Dashboard
    • Document the Management Review
    • Attach documents and use templates
    • Understand the Leader Sign-Off process

    Video Analytics

    Video has become one of the most engaging form of content. They can make information more digestible and easier to understand than a text article. But for most organizations, simply having video content available isn’t enough. You need to know if people are actually watching your videos.

    Insight into the viewing session

    Leveraging xAPI, I was able to measure the performance of the video, and gain insights into how viewers were interacting with our content.

    Drop-Off Points

    This metric provides a visual representation of where the majority of our viewers are dropping off, which provides an insight to what is happening at that moment in the video, or a ballpark for how long to aim for with our subsequent videos.

  • Kronos Workforce Timekeeper

    In preparation for an upgrade from the older version of Kronos (a workforce management software), the HR Operations, Payroll, Educators and I worked together to provide an understanding of the improvements and introduce new features.

    learning objectives

    This course provided instructions on managing employees’ timecards in Kronos to ensure documentation requirements are met and all employees are paid accurately by showing how to:

    • Navigate through Kronos
    • Add missed punches (with appropriate documentation)
    • Add missed transfers (department, position, and/or work rule)
    • Add non-productive pay codes to employees’ timecards
    • Understand the Attestation process

    RESULTS

    94%

    Of the learners were satisfied with the updated content

    97%

    Course completion rate
